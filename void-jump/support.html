Thanks for playing Void Jump. If something is not working, or you have a suggestion, drop a line to the address above.

What to include in a support request

  • A short description of what you tried to do and what happened instead.
  • Your platform (Android, iOS, Yandex Games web, RuStore, etc.).
  • Device model and OS version (for mobile).
  • Browser and OS (for web).
  • Your deviceUuid if it relates to leaderboard / progress — you can find it in the Settings screen of the App.
  • A screenshot or short screen recording, if relevant.

Response time

This is an independent project, so support is best-effort. Most messages are answered within five business days.

Common questions

Why did my score disappear?

Scores are tied to a per-device anonymous identifier. If you clear app data, reinstall the App, or play on a different device without signing in to a linked platform account (e.g. Yandex), the App treats you as a new player. Contact support with your old deviceUuid if you have it.

How do I delete my data?

Email the address above with your deviceUuid and we will remove the associated record. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Is the App free?

The App is free to download and play. On supported platforms it shows advertisements from third-party ad networks, and offers an optional in-app purchase that disables those advertisements. See the Privacy Policy for details.
