Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Void Jump mobile and web game ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you play it.

1. Who we are

The App is developed and operated by AndyGo Games (contact: andygo-erevan@gmail.com). The App is currently free, contains no advertisements, and offers no in-app purchases.

2. What we collect

  • Anonymous device identifier. On first launch we generate a random UUID (deviceUuid) and store it locally. We never read the Android Advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address, or any other persistent hardware identifier.
  • Display name. A short nickname shown on the leaderboard. By default it is an auto-generated placeholder; on the Yandex Games platform it may be sourced from your Yandex public profile if you choose to sign in.
  • Gameplay data. Final score and number of platforms cleared for each completed run.
  • Settings. Locale, sound on/off, music on/off, premium entitlement flags.

We do not collect: your real name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera input, or any health data.

3. How we use it

  • To show global, weekly, and per-platform leaderboards.
  • To synchronise your progress and settings across your devices.
  • To detect and mitigate cheating (input cadence and score plausibility checks).
  • To respond to support requests.

4. Where it is stored

Game data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL, hosted on AWS) and served via Cloudflare Workers. Both providers operate under their own privacy practices:

  • Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy

5. Third-party platforms

Depending on where you obtained the App, additional platform-level data handling applies:

  • Yandex Games
  • Google Play
  • RuStore

6. Sharing

Your display name and score may appear in publicly visible leaderboards within the App. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

7. Retention

We keep gameplay records for as long as the App is operated. You may request deletion of your record at any time (see Section 10).

8. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your rights

Subject to your local law (including the GDPR for EU residents), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the data associated with your deviceUuid. To exercise these rights, contact us at andygo-erevan@gmail.com and include the deviceUuid shown in the App's settings screen.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will be announced inside the App.

11. Contact

Questions? Email andygo-erevan@gmail.com.
