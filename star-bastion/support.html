Response time

This is an independent project, so support is best-effort. Most messages are answered within five business days.

Common questions

Why did my progress disappear?

Star Bastion stores all your progress - high score, cleared levels, credits, upgrades, weapons, and achievements - only on the device you play on. There is no online account or cloud sync, so if you clear the app data, reinstall the game, or switch to a different device or browser, the game starts you fresh. This is expected behaviour and cannot be recovered.

How do I delete my data?

All data is local to your device, and we keep no copy on any server. To erase it, clear the game's data in your browser or device settings, or simply uninstall the game. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Is the game free?

The game is free to download and play. On supported platforms it shows advertisements from third-party ad networks, and offers optional in-app purchases: a one-time ad-removal item and packs of in-game credits. See the Privacy Policy for details.
