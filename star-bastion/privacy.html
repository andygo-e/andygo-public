Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Star Bastion mobile and web game ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you play it.

1. Who we are

The App is developed and operated by AndyGo Games (contact: andygo-erevan@gmail.com). The App is free to download and play. On supported platforms it shows advertisements provided by third-party ad networks, and offers optional in-app purchases: a one-time item that disables advertisements, and consumable packs of in-game credits (see Sections 4 and 5).

2. What we collect

The App has no backend server and no player account. Everything below is stored only on your own device - we never receive or transmit it:

  • Gameplay progress. Your high score, which campaign levels and blocks you have cleared, and your fastest clear time per level.
  • In-game credits. Your current credits balance (the in-game wallet earned by playing or bought as a credit pack).
  • Owned items. The stat upgrades and weapons you have bought and upgraded, and the achievements you have earned.
  • Settings. Interface language, sound on/off, music on/off.
  • Purchase status. If you buy the ad-removal item, the App stores a boolean flag indicating that you own it. We do not receive your payment-method details - those are handled exclusively by the store (Yandex IAP on Yandex Games).

We do not collect: your real name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera input, any health data, or any persistent hardware identifier (we never read the Android Advertising ID, IMEI, or MAC address).

3. How we use it

  • To save your progress, credits, owned items, and settings on your device.
  • To unlock the ad-removal item and grant credit packs you purchase.
  • To show advertisements on supported platforms.
  • To respond to support requests.

The App has no leaderboards, no online accounts, and no cross-device sync.

4. Advertisements

On platforms where the App shows ads (currently Yandex Games advertising on the web build and Yandex Mobile Ads on Android; Google AdMob may be added in future releases), the embedded ad SDK independently collects a limited set of data on the device - typically a non-persistent advertising identifier, coarse approximate location, and basic device characteristics - to serve and measure ads. We do not have direct access to this data; we receive only aggregate revenue reports.

Ad-network data handling is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Yandex Games advertising
  • Yandex Mobile Ads SDK
  • Google AdMob / Google Ads

5. Purchases

The optional in-app purchases - the one-time ad-removal item and the consumable credit packs - are processed entirely by the platform store (the Yandex IAP API on Yandex Games). We receive a confirmation that your purchase succeeded, and the entitlement or credits are then granted locally on your device. We never see your bank card, billing address, or any payment-method information. Refunds are handled by the store under its own refund policy.

6. Where it is stored

All of the data in Section 2 is stored only on your device - in the browser's local storage on the web, or in the app's local preferences on Android. There is no server, database, or cloud sync: we do not upload, back up, or copy your data anywhere. Clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App removes it permanently.

7. Third-party platforms

Depending on where you obtained the App, additional platform-level data handling applies:

  • Yandex Games
  • Google Play
  • RuStore

8. Sharing

We do not collect personal data, so we have nothing to sell or rent to third parties. The ad networks listed in Section 4 handle the data they collect under their own privacy policies.

9. Retention

Because your data lives only on your device, it stays there until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App. We keep no copy.

10. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).

11. Your rights

Because all data is stored locally, it is entirely under your control: you can view or reset your progress inside the App, and you can erase it completely by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App - no request to us is needed. Subject to your local law (including the GDPR for EU residents), if you have any questions about your data, contact us at andygo-erevan@gmail.com.

12. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version. Material changes will be announced inside the App.

13. Contact

Questions? Email andygo-erevan@gmail.com.
